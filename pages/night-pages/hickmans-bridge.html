Hickman's Bridge

Where in the Park is this?

From Torry, travel east on Highway 24 for 11 miles, at which point you will see the Visitor Cetner. Stay on the highway, passing the turnoff to the Visitor Center. Shortly after passing the Visitor Center, you will see a small parking area for the Fruita Schoolhouse. Pass the turnouts for the Schoolhouse and the Petroglyph Panel. The Hickman Bridge trailhead is the next turnout of the left.
